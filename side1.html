What it's like to forget

"To forget would mean losing it more.
Never sharing the memories we shared, every sight we stared, every fight we'd fight.
Stuck in an amnesiacs memory, a conservationists delight."
- Alexander Pope

Forgetting your expereinces will cause them to repeat, never learning from what caused them in the first place. An endless loop of pain and grief, more than remembering the bad.
